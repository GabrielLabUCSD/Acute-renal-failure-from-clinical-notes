The patient has a body mass index (BMI) of 25, indicating he is within the normal weight range.

During the preoperative evaluation, the patient is advised to continue taking his medications up to the day of surgery. He is instructed to fast for 8 hours prior to the surgery and to avoid any blood-thinning medications or supplements for a week before the procedure.

The patient's recovery is uneventful, and he is discharged home on the second postoperative day.
