Prior to the surgery, the patient underwent a comprehensive preoperative evaluation, including routine blood work, an EKG, and a chest x-ray. All results were within normal limits, and the patient was deemed appropriate for surgery.

During the surgery, the patient received general anesthesia and the procedure was performed laparoscopically. The surgery went smoothly without any complications. No unexpected findings were noted during the procedure, and the hernia was successfully repaired.

The patient reported minimal discomfort and was satisfied with the outcome of the surgery. No complications were noted during this follow-up visit.
