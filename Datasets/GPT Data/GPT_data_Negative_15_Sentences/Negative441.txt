The patient has a history of hyperlipidemia, but his current lipid profile is within normal limits due to his adherence to statin therapy. He has no history of pancreatitis or gallbladder issues.

In terms of surgical preparation, the patient is asked to discontinue the dual antiplatelet therapy one week before the surgery. He is advised to stop smoking and avoid alcohol intake for at least two weeks prior to the procedure. He undergoes preoperative evaluation and is deemed fit for surgery.
