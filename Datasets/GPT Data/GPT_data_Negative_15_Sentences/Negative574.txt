The patient's wound was assessed regularly for signs of infection or delayed healing. No complications were noted, and the incision site showed signs of appropriate healing.

In terms of complications, the patient did not experience any major postoperative complications such as myocardial infarction, cardiac arrhythmias, or respiratory compromise. His pain was managed effectively with appropriate analgesia, and he did not require any additional interventions.
