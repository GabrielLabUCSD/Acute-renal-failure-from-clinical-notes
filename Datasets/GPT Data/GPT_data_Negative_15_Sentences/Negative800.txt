In preparation for surgery, the patient underwent a thorough preoperative assessment, including blood work, imaging studies, and consultations with various specialists to optimize his medical conditions. His medications were carefully reviewed and adjusted as needed to reduce the risk of perioperative complications.

Postoperatively, the patient was closely monitored in the recovery room for any signs of infection, bleeding, or complications related to anesthesia. He was started on pain medications and physical therapy to aid in his recovery and rehabilitation process.
