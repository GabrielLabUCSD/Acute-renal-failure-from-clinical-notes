In terms of medications, Mr. X is currently taking lisinopril for hypertension, metformin for diabetes, and occasional antihistamines for his seasonal allergies. He does not take any herbal supplements or over-the-counter medications regularly.

Mr. X was discharged from the hospital five days after the surgery in a stable condition. He was given appropriate instructions regarding medication management, wound care, and follow-up appointments.
