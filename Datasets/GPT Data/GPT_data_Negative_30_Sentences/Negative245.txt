His cardiovascular examination revealed no significant abnormalities, and he had no history of cardiac arrhythmias or heart failure. In terms of postoperative complications, the patient did not experience any significant complications. He did not develop any wound infections, deep vein thrombosis, pulmonary embolism, or cardiac complications. There were no signs of surgical site bleeding or hematoma formation. The patient's overall recovery was considered successful, and he was discharged home in stable condition.
