The patient's incision site was monitored for signs of infection and proper wound healing. He was instructed on proper wound care techniques, including the importance of keeping the incision area clean and dry.

The patient's primary care physician will continue to manage his chronic conditions and monitor his blood pressure, cholesterol levels, and blood sugar control in the postoperative period.
