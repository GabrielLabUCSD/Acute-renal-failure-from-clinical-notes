On the fifth day, the patient's pain levels were minimal, and he was able to manage them effectively with oral pain medications. The plan was to discharge the patient home the following day, as he had met all the necessary recovery milestones.

His postoperative follow-up appointments were scheduled appropriately to monitor his progress and ensure his continued recovery.
