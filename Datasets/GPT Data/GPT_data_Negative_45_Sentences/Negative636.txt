His kidney function tests are normal, with an estimated glomerular filtration rate (eGFR) within the normal range.

No immediate complications arise during the immediate postoperative period. The patient does not experience any postoperative bleeding, wound infection, or respiratory distress. He is monitored closely for signs of infection, such as fever or increased surgical site tenderness, and receives appropriate antibiotic prophylaxis.

The patient's surgical wound demonstrates proper healing and remains free from signs of infection. He is discharged home on postoperative day three with appropriate postoperative instructions, including wound care and activity restrictions.

No complications related to the surgery or anesthesia are identified during the follow-up visits. The patient's glycemic control, as assessed by his blood sugar readings, remains stable without any significant fluctuations.
