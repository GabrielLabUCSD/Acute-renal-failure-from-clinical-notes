His renal function is within normal limits, with a serum creatinine of 0.9 mg/dL and an estimated glomerular filtration rate (eGFR) of 80 mL/min.

He has completed a preoperative evaluation, including a chest X-ray, electrocardiogram, and basic metabolic panel. The results showed no significant abnormalities that would contraindicate surgery.

Following the surgery, the patient's postoperative course was uneventful. He was managed with appropriate pain control, including NSAIDs and opioids as needed. On the fifth day post-surgery, the patient was discharged home with instructions for home care and postoperative physical therapy. He was advised to continue his regular medications and to follow up with his primary care physician for further management of his chronic conditions.
