Throughout his recovery, Patient X will be closely followed by the surgical team and the nursing staff. His vital signs, pain levels, and wound healing will be monitored regularly. A physical therapist may be involved to assist with ambulation and rehabilitation if necessary.

The patient tolerated the surgery well and was able to resume a regular diet and oral medications without any issues.

Patient X's postoperative course was uneventful, with appropriate wound healing and gradual improvement in ambulation.
