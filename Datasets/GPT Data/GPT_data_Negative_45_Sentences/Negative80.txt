The patient's surgical incision site remains clean and shows signs of adequate healing in the subsequent follow-up visits. There are no signs of infection or wound dehiscence.

Upon discharge, the patient is educated about potential signs of postoperative complications and is advised to closely monitor their incision site for any abnormalities. He is given a prescription for pain medication and advised to continue his regular medications as prescribed.
