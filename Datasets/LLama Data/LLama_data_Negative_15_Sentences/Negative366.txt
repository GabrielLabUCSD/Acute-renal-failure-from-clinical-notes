His electrolytes, creatinine, and liver enzymes are within normal limits.  A preoperative ECG and echocardiogram were performed, showing no abnormalities.

He ambulated independently and did not require respiratory therapy.  He had a uneventful postoperative course and was discharged home on the fifth postoperative day.
