He has no known allergies and has not had any previous surgeries.

The patient"s surgical plan is to undergo a lumbar decompression and fusion surgery.

Preoperative medications include:

* Aspirin 325mg po daily
* Lisinopril 40mg po daily
* Simvastatin 40mg po daily
* Ibuprofen 800mg po every 6 hours as needed for pain

Preoperative lab results:

* WBC 11,000/mm3
* Hemoglobin 14.2g/dL

The patient had an uneventful preoperative course.

* He was also advised to stop smoking and to follow a healthy diet.
