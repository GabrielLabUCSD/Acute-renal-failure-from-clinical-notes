The patient's MRI reveals a large L4-L5 disc herniation with severe spinal stenosis and bilateral neural foraminal stenosis. The patient's symptoms are consistent with a degenerative disc disease.

The patient has been scheduled for an L4-L5 laminectomy and fusion surgery. The surgical plan includes a posterior approach with decompression of the spinal cord and nerve roots, as well as fusion with instrumentation.
