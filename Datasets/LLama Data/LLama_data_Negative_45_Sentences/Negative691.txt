The patient is allergic to penicillin and has a known allergy to latex.

Surgical History:

The patient has a history of appendectomy and cholecystectomy.

The patient underwent a successful lumbar fusion surgery. He was extubated in the operating room and was transferred to the recovery room in stable condition. The patient did not experience any major complications during his hospital stay. Follow-up:

The patient followed up with his surgeon 2 weeks after discharge. His wound was healing well, and he reported a significant improvement in his back pain. He was advised to continue with his postoperative medications and follow a regular exercise program to improve his mobility.

Conclusion:

This patient summary provides a comprehensive overview of the patient's medical history, surgical procedure, and postoperative course.
