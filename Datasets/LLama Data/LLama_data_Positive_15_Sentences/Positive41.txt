The patient's urine output was monitored closely and showed improvement after dialysis.

The patient's blood cultures grew E. coli and were treated with appropriate antibiotics.

The patient's sepsis improved, and he was weaned off vasopressors.

The patient's ARF improved, and he was able to stop dialysis.

The patient was transferred to the ICU for close monitoring.

The patient's recovery was uneventful, and he was discharged home after 2 weeks.

The patient followed up for regular check-ups and had normal renal function.
